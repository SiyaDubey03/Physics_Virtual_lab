🔬 Newton's Rings Setup

Apparatus: Plano-convex lens, plane glass plate, sodium light (λ=589nm), microscope.

📖 Procedure

  1. Place lens on glass plate, illuminate with monochromatic source.
  2. Observe concentric circular interference fringes.
  3. Move virtual slider to measure diameter of dark rings.
  4. Calculate wavelength or radius of curvature.

📖 Procedure

⚡ Four Probe Method

Setup: Four equally spaced probes on semiconductor sample, current source, voltmeter. Resistivity ρ = (V/I) × 2πS (for thin film correction).

📖 Procedure

  1. Apply constant current I between outer probes.
  2. Measure voltage V across inner probes.
  3. Probe spacing S = 2 mm.
  4. Calculate resistivity using formula.
